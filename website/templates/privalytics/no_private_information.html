How does Privalytics work

We don't store Private Information

Compared to other analytics solutions, we don't need to store personal information about your users, this makes us GDPR compliant by definition. We only store metrics about how your website is used, where the visitors come from and the type of device they use. Nothing of which can trace back to an individual user.

We don't use cookies

By choosing not to use cookies, you don't need to show the cookie banner to your users. Even if underrated, many users online feel more confident visiting websites which don't force them to allow cookies. The downside of not using cookies is that we can't provide precise metrics on the number of pages a single person visits, or the exact path they followed in a given session.

We respect do not track

Many users set the do not track option on their browsers. We take care of this by only registering the page visited, but nothing extra that belongs to the real user, such as the browser, location or referrer. We follow the guidelines of the W3C and implement extra restrictions in order to avoid any mixed feelings with your users.

About Location Information

We provide location information through https://www.maxmind.com GeoLite2 database. In order to avoid information leakage, we store the database locally and only keep the information of the country of origin. This makes it virtually impossible to trace back the identity of a user based on the location.

About User Agent and Operating System

There is a wealth of possible combinations of operating systems and user agents. More specifically, every browser posses a build number that changes with each update. The combination of operating system and specific browser used can be used to identify users. That is why we only store the browser brand (Firefox, Opera, etc.) and operating system (Linux, Windows, etc.).

Doubts or suggestions?

We are continuously trying to improve our product. Drop us a line to contact@privalytics.io if you would like to discuss how to implement safer mechanisms or more detailed analytics.
