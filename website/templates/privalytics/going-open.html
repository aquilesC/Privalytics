Going Completely Open

At Privalytics we are committed to transparency and continuous learning. That is why we decided to go fully open. On Github you will find not only the code to build the website, but also all our plans to build a company. How we follow the lean approach, what experiments do we perform and what ideas do we validate.

We hope that Privalytics can become a resource for starting entrepreneurs who want to learn from a real example. Supporting Privalytics not only makes a better internet, it also generates a growing learning resource for everybody who wants to follow the entrepreneurial path.
