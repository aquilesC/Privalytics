We are Open

At Privalytics we are committed to transparency and continuous learning. That is why we decided to go fully open. The code that builds this website is hosted on Github, and also the roadmap of new feature we plan on releasing. Do you have a killer idea? Suggest it and we will consider adding it to our product!

We hope that Privalytics can become a resource for starting entrepreneurs who want to learn from a real example. Supporting Privalytics not only makes a better internet, it also generates a growing learning resource for everybody who wants to follow the entrepreneurial path.
